The first monsoon showers brought back the sparkling glow of the city by beating the scorching heat and pollution of summer. But, are these droplets going to help our skin glow? They don’t seem to be much skin friendly. During monsoon the dust particles and dirt settles on our skin and creates multiple skin issues like acne, blackheads, pimples etc. Then why not choose the right foods this wet season to protect our skin?

Let’s take a look at these yummy skin friendly items:

  • 1) Yoghurt – Yoghurt is a good source of protein. It helps in maintaining the moisture in skin. Proteins are known for its anti – ageing properties, hence yoghurt in form of fruit yoghurt or yoghurt with muesli can be a perfect snack for monsoon.
  • 2) Lemon honey Water – Lemon (rich source of Vitamin –C) is an age old remedy for skin problems! Hence, it is rightly said that it helps in formation of collagen (in skin) making it look brighter and healthy. Having lemon honey water in monsoon can help in maintaining glow on the skin and also helps as body cleanser
  • 3) Tomatoes- Tomatoes are rich in Lycopene. There has been a few evidence that lycopene may help in lowering acne promoting hormone. Being an antioxidant, lycopene helps in protecting skin from free radicals.
  • 4) Green tea – sipping a nice of green tea in monsoon is very comforting to the body. It releases stress from the body and also helps in preventing skin irritation and Psoriasis.
  • 5) Dates- It is difficult to choose palak (good source of iron) in monsoon as green leafy vegetables spoil faster; hence dates are handy options to make sure the skin doesn’t turn pale.
  • 6) Assorted fruits – litchi, cherries, pomegranate, plums etc are readily available in monsoon. They are good source of carotenoids and help in hydration of our skin.
