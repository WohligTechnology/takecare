What is health design ?

Only the fit know how fabulous it feels.

Health Design at Selfcare means redesigning your life, nutritiously. We teach you to know your body better and empower you to make the right choices for a healthier lifestyle. At Selfcare, we give priority to your overall health and design programs keeping your medical and genetic history in mind. Under the expert guidance of SumanAgarwal, an Oxford qualified clinical nutritionist as well as a fitness expert each health plan is created to fit your healthgoals, be it weight-loss, ailment control, weight gain, pregnancy or anti-aging programs,without compromising on lifestyle.

Health is, after all, about a state of absolute harmony - of the body, mind and spirit.
