know more about tina

An AICI consultant Tina is a Personal Image consultant, Corporate Speaker and Personal Stylist. She has a masters degree in Luxury Marketing and Management from France. She has practiced her Image and Grooming skills in the luxury industry in fashion across the globe in France, Germany, China and Hong Kong. Tina hopes to empower people by teaching them the power of Image & Personal Branding.

Her personal styling services caters to clients at all ages from 14 years onwards men, women and younger girls. Her services involve personal styling for men and women, bridal and trousseau services as well as corporate image management.

Her recent achievements include; A diploma in Personal Branding & Menswear from the prestigious Image Professional Institute - NY, USA.

Adding to this she is now part of the faculty at Fashion institute and representative of UK-Nottingham in Mumbai Pearl Academy.
